🎮 How To Play
Choose Your Fighter - Each has unique stats, moves, and passives
Select Moves - Consider tags, cooldowns, and enemy state
Manage Meters - High Chaos = unpredictable; High Clarity = victory
Discover Combos - Chain specific moves for bonus effects
Exploit Weaknesses - Use Speed against Sloppy, Stealth against Projectile
Win - By any means necessary
Tag Interaction Cheat Sheet
Your Tag	vs Enemy's Last Tag	Result
Speed	Sloppy	+50% damage 💪
Stealth	Sloppy	+30% damage
Projectile	Sloppy	+40% damage
Speed	Stealth	-10% damage 😬
Projectile	Stealth	-30% damage
